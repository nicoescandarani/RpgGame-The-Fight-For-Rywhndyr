The Fight For Rywhndyr.

The kingdom of Rywhndyr has been divided since Dagast The Mad killed the last just king and murdered all of his family and lords. The people couldn´t tollerate it and reveled. A civil war between the highest families and the new king began and soon all became to ashes. Since that great war, this lands are filled with crime and social insecurity. New leaders rise, but with power comes great tempation. They are called The Four Leaders, and they will need to be stoped to secure the crown. We need a new king. Are you the one?

You will need to pick a general. I´ve provided you already with the best of the kingdom. Please, kill all of the four leaders and become our new hope.
